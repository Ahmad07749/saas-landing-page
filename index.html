FAQ
Questions we get asked a lot
Do I need a credit card to start my free trial?+
No card required. Sign up with your email, explore every feature for 14 days, and only add payment details if you decide to continue. We remind you three days before the trial ends.
Can I switch plans as my team grows?+
Yes — upgrade or downgrade any time from your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. We don't lock you into anything.
What integrations are available?+
200+ tools including Slack, HubSpot, Salesforce, GitHub, Jira, Notion, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Stripe, and more. We also offer a REST API and a Zapier connector for custom integrations.
How secure is FlowDesk?+
SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. Data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Enterprise plans include SSO, SCIM, IP allowlisting, and full audit logs.
What does AI Copilot actually do?+
It drafts email replies, summarizes threads, suggests automation rules, and flags anomalies. Context-aware and learns from your workspace. No data is used to train external models.
Can I cancel any time?+
Yes. Cancel from billing settings any time. You keep access until the end of your billing period. We don't make it hard to find — it's in plain sight in your account.
